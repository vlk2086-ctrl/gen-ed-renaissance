Community Contest

Design our Renaissance

Help give the General Education Renaissance a visual identity. Submit artwork, illustration, or design concepts that capture what this moment means for SCAD — open to students, faculty, and staff.

Prompt

What we're looking for

A visual that captures renewal, curiosity, and human-centered progress — our Sistine-Chapel-meets-modern-studio idea of what a "renaissance" looks like at SCAD. Any medium: illustration, typography, photography, motion, mixed media.

Eligibility

Who can enter

Open to all SCAD students, faculty, and staff. Individual or team submissions are welcome. One primary image per entry, plus a short description.

Use

How winning work is used

The selected concept(s) may be adapted as the visual identity for General Education Renaissance communications, this toolkit site, and campus materials. Questions about rights or usage: curriculum@scad.edu.

Guidelines

File size 5MB or less. JPG, PNG, or GIF work best for preview. By submitting, you confirm this is your own original work and that it may be shared publicly on this site and in project communications.

Have a larger file or a different format (video, PDF portfolio)? Email it directly to curriculum@scad.edu.
